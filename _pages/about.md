Welcome to Cmdlets and Coffee: Your New Brew for PowerShell Mastery!
Howdy, fellow IT Pros and system administrators!

If you're reading this, chances are you've spent your fair share of time wrestling with the command line, automating tasks, and perhaps even enjoying a good cup of coffee while doing it. If that sounds like you, then you've found your new favorite spot: welcome to Cmdlets and Coffee!

This isn't just a blog; it's the home base for our brand-new podcast, dedicated to helping professionals like you truly master PowerShell. Whether you're in Houston, like me, or anywhere else in the world, my goal is simple: to transform you from a basic scripter into a professional developer.

Why Cmdlets and Coffee?

I've been in the trenches, just like you. I've seen firsthand how powerful PowerShell can be, and also how frustrating it can be when you hit a wall or when you're just not sure how to take your scripts to the next level. I started this podcast because I believe every IT pro deserves clear, practical guidance to build scalable, secure, and resilient automation.

Over a virtual cup of coffee, we'll demystify complex PowerShell topics and teach you the mindset and practices required for modern, enterprise-level automation. We're going beyond just learning commands; we're learning to think like a PowerShell Pro.

What Can You Expect?

Each week, the Cmdlets and Coffee podcast will deliver engaging, actionable content. We'll be covering:

The Foundation: Getting started with PowerShell, understanding the pipeline, variables, and how to navigate the "PowerShell Universe" with PSDrives.

Professional Practices: Elevating your game with Git for version control, mastering Pester for testing, setting up your VS Code environment, and securing your scripts.

Advanced Concepts: Diving deep into building your own PowerShell modules, using classes, integrating with cloud services like Microsoft Graph and Azure Key Vault, and much more.

On this blog, you'll find much more than just episode announcements. For every podcast episode, you'll get:

Detailed Show Notes: All the links, resources, and key takeaways discussed.

Full Transcripts: Making our content accessible and searchable.

Code Snippets: Easily copy-and-paste the examples right into your console.

Bonus Articles & Tutorials: Extra content to help you explore topics in more depth.
